Potato pancake

Description

These potato pancakes with cheese are crispy on the outside and soft and melty in the middle, fast and easy for a late night snack.

Perfect to cutomize to taste or a great way to use the potatoes sitting in the pantry.

Ingredients

  • 1 russet potato
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of oniion powder
  • Pinch of garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded or cubed)
  • Oil

Steps

  • Step 1, peel and dice the potato and put it to boil in a saucepan for 15 min.
  • Step 2, put the potato in a large bowl, add the seasoning and smash everything together it should have the same consistency as smashed potatoes.
  • step 3, seperate to potato mixuture into fifth and roll into balls, flatten each ball and add a handfull of cheese to the middle then close the ball so no cheese comes out and flatten a little again.
  • Step 4, add a generous amout of oil to a pan, then on medium high heat add the pancakes to the pan for 4 min on each side or until golden brown.
  • Step 5, let cool for 3 min then enjoy with a dippindg sauce or alone
