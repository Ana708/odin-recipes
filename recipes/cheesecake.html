Cheesecake

Description

This cheesecake is a creamy and rich dessert and makes a perfect for a fast and easy snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups graham crackers
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 can condesed milk
  • 16 oz sour cream
  • 8 oz cream cheese
